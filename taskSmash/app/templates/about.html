About TaskSmash

TaskSmash is a modern approach to managing your to-dos where tasks come to life in a social and creative way.

What is TaskSmash?

TaskSmash is designed to transform mundane task management into an engaging experience. Whether you're tracking personal goals or collaborating with others, TaskSmash offers a unique blend of practicality and social interaction.

Tools and Technologies

This application is built using:

  • Flask – A micro web framework in Python that makes it easy to build web applications.
  • SQLAlchemy – For object-relational mapping (ORM) to interact with the database.
  • Flask-Login – To manage user authentication and sessions.
  • Jinja2 – For templating and rendering dynamic HTML pages.
  • Mailgun API – For sending emails and managing password resets.

Why TaskSmash?

Beyond basic task management, TaskSmash is about socializing your to-dos. From following other users to engaging with their tasks through comments and replies, this app turns productivity into a collaborative and fun experience.

Practical and Fun Uses

TaskSmash is perfect for:

  • Boosting personal productivity with a social twist.
  • Collaborating with friends or colleagues on shared task lists.
  • Transforming routine task management into a creative, interactive experience.

We believe that a bit of creativity and community can make even the most ordinary tasks exciting. Welcome to TaskSmash, ToDo Done Different!
